About Me

Hello! Thanks so much for stopping by my website.

As you (hopefully) know by this point, my name is Kelsey Eckelberry, and I love to create. I graduated from John Brown University in 2015 with a Bachelors of Science in Graphic & Web Design / Photography.

I've been interested in fine art from a young age, but I never thought that I could have a future as an artist. My sophomore year of high school was a turning point because I was introduced to the wonderful world of digital art. I took a class that introduced me to design of almost every kind and I truly began to grasp a larger understanding of art as a whole. Instead of using brushes, paint, and a canvas, I realized I could create art with photography, graphic design, and typography.
